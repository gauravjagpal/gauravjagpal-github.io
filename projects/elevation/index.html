About Elevation

Here at Elevation, we say goodbye to quick fixes and hello to lasting change. Whatever obstacles you may have faced in the past, we are here to help by giving you all the tools you need to finally get you on the path to sustainably reaching your goals.

The key to achieving your fitness goals is creating the right habits which train your mind to achieve what you set out to AND building your body up to perform optimally during your workouts and throughout the day - that's why here at Elevation, we give you all the tools you need to make your goals a reality - all you need to do is put in the work!

Whether you just need a little help structuring your workouts and knowing what your diet should look like, or you'd like more holistic support, someone to ask your questions to and help you stay accountable as you progress along you're journey, we've got the plan to cover it. Fill out the enquiry form to submit your interest and i'll reach out to you to get started.

How Can Elevation Help You:

Basic Plan:

  • Background info to help you understand the 'why' behind your plan
  • 6 week workout plan (gym-based)
  • Nutritional guidance (ie daily calorie and macro goals)
  • 7 day sample meal plan
  • Background info pack
  • Initial 7 day email support or option to request changes to plan

Premium Plan:

  • All the same as the basic plan PLUS:
  • Initial 30-45 minute coaching call to get clearer on your unique goals, any obstacles you've faced in the past and any worries or concerns you may have, allowing a highly specific plan for you
  • Wellness coaching to aid your fitness journey
  • Mid-way check-in call to check on progress, address queries and make any necessary amendments to plan
  • 24/7 support (via email or whatsapp) including option to share workout videos for professional coaching feedback and tips on form, tempo etc
